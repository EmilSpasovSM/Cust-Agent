title: Reusable packaging systems and reverse logistics
source: Internal training note
date: 2025-03-03
doc_id: local:doc_pack_03

Reusable shippers and mailers reduce single-use waste when they achieve sufficient reuse cycles.

Key operational requirement: reverse logistics. Deposit incentives can raise return rates, but they add customer friction and require tracking, cleaning, and inspection.

Organizations often pilot reusable packaging in dense delivery regions first, where return shipping distances are shorter and collection is easier.
