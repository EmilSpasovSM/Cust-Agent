title: EPR reporting and recycled content targets
source: Internal training note
date: 2025-02-19
doc_id: local:doc_pack_06

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) policies can increase fees for hard-to-recycle packaging and require more detailed reporting by material type.

Companies respond by simplifying packaging (mono-materials), increasing recycled content, and improving documentation of packaging specifications.

Operationally, teams often build packaging data catalogs so sustainability, procurement, and compliance share a single source of truth.
